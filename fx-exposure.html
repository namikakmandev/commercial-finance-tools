Net ExposureWhere you're actually exposed

A company with 50% EUR revenue and 50% EUR costs has zero net EUR exposure — the moves cancel. Net exposure shows what currencies actually move your margin. Long = revenue outweighs costs (you benefit when that currency strengthens). Short = costs outweigh revenue (you lose when that currency strengthens).

How to Read This

The "Realized" panel uses real exchange rate history to show what FX moves actually did to your margin over the chosen window, given your stated exposure.

The "Stress" panel is forward-looking but does not predict FX. You shock each currency by a chosen %, and the tool runs the math on your exposure. No model, no forecast.

The "Volatility" panel measures historical risk — not future risk. A currency that's been quiet for 12 months can still move sharply tomorrow.

This tool does not give financial advice. Hedge hints are mechanical thresholds, not recommendations. Talk to your treasury team or bank before any actual hedging.
